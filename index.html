13 million artifacts. Allow 2–3 hrs. Must-sees: Rosetta Stone, Egyptian mummies, Elgin Marbles, Lewis Chessmen. Pre-book free timed entry at britishmuseum.org — gets very busy.

Free · Van Gogh, da Vinci, Rembrandt
›
🚶 10 min walk from Covent Garden

›
🚶 5 min walk from National Gallery
47 Chandos Place. Small, narrow, atmospheric. Excellent cask ales. Standing room spills onto the street on nice evenings. London institution — been here for decades unchanged.

Street art, vintage, great food — most unique area

🚇 Fulham Broadway → Earl's Court → Liverpool St (Central) 🚶 10 min walk to Shoreditch

Contactless bank card works on all TfL — same as Oyster, no setup needed

Automatically applied — you'll never overpay regardless of how many trips

~12:30am weekdays, ~1:30am weekends
Uber
Works well for late nights but avoid after Wembley or big events — surge is brutal
